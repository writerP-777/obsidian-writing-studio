## Welcome to Obsidian Writing Studio Discussions

Obsidian Writing Studio is a plugin built for writers — and writers have a lot to talk about. This Discussions space is the right place for conversations that go beyond a bug report or a feature request.

**What belongs here:**

- **Questions** — How do I set up a sprint goal for an entire project? Can I use Typography Mode with a custom vault theme? If it is not clearly a bug, ask it here.
- **Workflows** — Share how you have structured your projects, which writing modes you use for which tasks, or how Writing Studio fits into your broader Obsidian setup.
- **Export & publishing** — Tips, Pandoc configurations, WordPress setup experiences, and format-specific advice.
- **Feature ideas** — Early-stage ideas you want to think through with the community before filing a formal feature request.
- **Show and tell** — Share something you have built or written using Writing Studio. Project structures, binder setups, export templates — all welcome.

**What belongs in Issues instead:**
If you have found a reproducible bug or want to make a formal feature request, please use the [issue templates](../../issues/new/choose) rather than Discussions. That keeps actionable items where they can be tracked and prioritized.
